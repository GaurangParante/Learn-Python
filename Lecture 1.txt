#Variables

#Assignment operators

#Rules for Identifiers (Variable Name)
1. Identifiers can be combination uppercase and lowercase letters, Digits or an underscore(_).
2. An Identifiers can not start with digit. 
3. We can't use special symbols like !,@,#,$,% ets in our Identifier.
4. Identifier can be of any length
